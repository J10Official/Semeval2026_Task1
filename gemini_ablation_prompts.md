## Task A1 — Headline-Based Jokes (2 items)

### Prompt 1 (en_2272)

```
Write a funny joke inspired by the following news headline. The joke should be original, creative, and no longer than 50 words.

Headline: "Why an AI 'godfather' is quitting Meta after 12 years"
```

The AI godfather left Meta because he finally realized "The Social Network" wasn't a training manual for a benevolent deity. He’s now looking for a job with less data-mining and more actual wine—turns out, turning water into algorithms is just exhausting for the soul.

### Prompt 2 (en_2002)

```
Write a funny joke inspired by the following news headline. The joke should be original, creative, and no longer than 50 words.

Headline: "Carra: Liverpool must sign in January - even after summer spending spree"
```
Liverpool’s board is terrified. They spent millions this summer, yet Jamie Carragher is still demanding more signings.

At this point, it’s not about the league title; they just need to buy enough players to physically block Jamie from entering the TV studio.
